**Full description:**

You already know AI can save you hours a week. The hard part is knowing
*what to actually type* — and most prompt lists online are generic filler
that don't hold up past the first try.

This playbook is 25 prompts built specifically for the tasks small business
owners and solo operators actually deal with every week:

- **Marketing & Content** — batch a week of social posts, rewrite testimonials, outline SEO blog posts, generate ad copy variants, turn notes into a newsletter
- **Customer Service & Support** — draft replies to upset customers, build an FAQ from real tickets, write policy pages that don't sound like a lawyer wrote them, triage tickets, respond to reviews
- **Finance & Operations** — categorize expenses, write plain-English cash flow updates, summarize vendor contracts, turn a process you do from memory into a real SOP, turn meeting notes into action items
- **Hiring & HR** — write job descriptions that don't scare off good candidates, build interview questions that actually test for the skill, score candidates fairly, onboard new hires, draft honest performance reviews
- **Sales & Outreach** — personalize cold emails without faking it, handle real objections, write proposals that reference the client's actual problem, build follow-up sequences, debrief wins and losses

Every prompt comes with **when to actually use it** and **a tip for adapting
it to your business** — because a prompt you copy-paste without customizing
is worth about as much as the generic list you didn't buy.

**What you get:**
- A 13-page PDF playbook, organized and ready to copy-paste
- A companion Excel/Google Sheets tracker with a built-in ROI calculator — log the prompts you use and see the actual hours (and dollars) saved
- Works with Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, or any AI chat assistant you already use
